# Overseed Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026

Welcome to Overseed! Our mission is to foster a transparent, professional, and creative ecosystem where brands and content creators can build meaningful cross-border partnerships. These Community Guidelines ("Guidelines") apply to all users, including brands, merchants, and content creators (collectively, "Users"), on the Overseed platform (the "Platform").

By using Overseed, you agree to uphold these standards. These Guidelines complement our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

Overseed is designed for professional collaboration. Whether you are a brand looking to expand globally or a creator looking for your next big partnership, these Guidelines ensure a safe and productive environment for everyone. We expect all Users to act with integrity, respect, and professionalism at all times.

## 2. Content Standards & Online Safety

All content shared on Overseed—including profiles, campaign briefs, messages, and promotional materials—must meet the following standards:

*   **Honesty & Accuracy:** Represent yourself and your work truthfully. Do not use fake followers, bot-inflated engagement metrics, or misleading portfolio pieces.
*   **No Misleading Content:** Do not post deceptive information about products, services, or campaign requirements.
*   **Intellectual Property (IP):** Respect the rights of others. Only upload content that you own or have the explicit permission to use.
*   **Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech:** We do not permit harassment, discrimination, or threatening behavior based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, age, gender identity, or sexual orientation.
*   **Safety & Decency:** No explicit, adult, or pornographic content. No depictions of extreme violence, illegal acts, or dangerous activities.
*   **Online Safety (UK Users):** In compliance with the **UK Online Safety Act 2023**, Overseed has duties to protect users from illegal content and priority harms. We take proactive measures to monitor and remove content that may be harmful to our community.

## 3. Advertising & Disclosure Requirements

Transparency is the foundation of influencer marketing. All creators using Overseed must comply with the advertising laws of their target markets and the markets where they reside.

*   **Legal Compliance:** You must adhere to the **FTC Endorsement Guides** (USA), the **ASA/CAP Code** (UK), **Ad Standards Canada**, and **China’s Advertising Law**, as applicable.
*   **China Advertising Law Specifics:** 
    - **Prohibited Terms:** Users are strictly prohibited from using absolute terms such as "best" (最好的), "most excellent" (最优秀的), "national level" (国家级), or similar superlatives that violate China's Advertising Law.
    - **Health Claims:** Any medical, healthcare, or pharmaceutical claims require prior regulatory approval and must not be made by individuals or entities without the proper authorization.
*   **Clear Disclosures:** Paid partnerships must be clearly and conspicuously disclosed. Use labels like `#ad`, `#sponsored`, or the platform’s built-in disclosure tools. Disclosures must be easily visible and not buried in hashtags.
*   **Substantiated Claims:** Do not make false or unverified claims about a product’s efficacy, safety, or health benefits. Brands are responsible for providing creators with accurate information.

## 4. Fair Dealing

We expect mutual respect between brands and creators to ensure successful collaborations.

*   **Brand Responsibilities:** Brands must provide accurate product descriptions and campaign goals. Samples must be shipped promptly, and payments must be issued according to the agreed-upon schedule.
*   **Creator Responsibilities:** Creators must deliver content that meets the agreed-upon brief and timeline. Any delays must be communicated to the brand immediately.
*   **Platform Integrity:** Users are prohibited from circumventing Overseed to avoid platform fees. Do not move active campaign discussions or payments off-platform.

## 5. Prohibited Conduct

The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account termination:

*   **Fraud & Misrepresentation:** Creating fake accounts, impersonating others, or engaging in fraudulent transactions.
*   **Metric Manipulation:** Using "engagement pods," buying likes/followers, or manipulating platform reviews.
*   **Harassment:** Stalking, bullying, or intimidating other Users.
*   **Confidentiality Breaches:** Sharing sensitive campaign details, pricing, or private messages without explicit consent.
*   **Illegal Activity:** Using the Platform to facilitate any criminal act.

## 6. Enforcement

Overseed reserves the right to maintain the quality and safety of our community. Enforcement actions may include:

*   Removal or editing of non-compliant content.
*   Formal warnings.
*   Temporary account suspension.
*   Permanent bans for severe or repeated violations.

Overseed has sole discretion in making enforcement decisions. 

**Reporting Violations:** If you encounter content or behavior that violates these Guidelines, please report it via the in-app reporting tools or email us at **report@overseed.net**.

**Appeals:** If you believe your account was moderated in error, you may submit an appeal to **community@overseed.net** within 14 days of the action.
