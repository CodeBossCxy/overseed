# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Overseed (hereinafter "Overseed," "we," or "the Platform") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information.

This Policy applies to the Overseed website, accounts, platform features, brief systems, in-platform messaging, AI tools, data analytics, and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Account Information
Name, email, password, avatar, account type, login method, contact information, etc.

### User Profile Information
Brand profiles, company information, creator profiles, social media accounts, location, industry, rates, portfolio, audience information, etc.

### Brief and Collaboration Information
Product information, collaboration requirements, budget, creator applications, collaboration status, deliverables, campaign reports, feedback, and related records.

### Communication Information
In-platform messages, customer service records, email correspondence, translated content, feedback, and complaint content.

### Payment and Transaction Information
Payment, settlement, withdrawal, identity verification, tax forms, and payment risk control information may be processed by Stripe or other third-party payment service providers. Overseed typically does not store full bank card information, but may store payment status, transaction IDs, billing records, settlement status, refund/chargeback records, and necessary payment metadata.

### Device and Usage Information
IP address, device type, browser, operating system, pages visited, click records, referring pages, logs, approximate location, etc.

### Cookie and Tracking Information
Information collected through cookies, pixels, SDKs, tags, and similar technologies. See Cookie Policy for details.

### AI Tool Input and Output Information
Product descriptions, images, briefs, scripts, translation text, prompts, generated results, and usage records that you submit to AI tools.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Create, verify, and manage accounts;
- Provide the Overseed platform and related features;
- Display brand, merchant, and creator profiles and collaboration opportunities;
- Support brief posting, applications, matching, communication, translation, and recommendations;
- Provide AI tools and generated content;
- Process subscriptions, payments, invoices, settlements, and related records;
- Send account notifications, service reminders, and operational messages;
- Send marketing communications where permitted by law;
- Analyze platform usage and improve services;
- Maintain security, prevent fraud, abuse, and violations;
- Fulfill legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, or law enforcement requirements;
- Process user requests, complaints, disputes, and contract enforcement.

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing Personal Information

Where UK GDPR or similar laws apply, we may process personal information based on the following grounds:

- **Performance of a Contract:** To provide you with accounts, the platform, and related services;
- **Legitimate Interests:** To operate, protect, improve, and promote Overseed, prevent fraud and abuse, and support user collaborations;
- **Consent:** For certain cookies, marketing communications, optional features, or scenarios where applicable law requires separate consent;
- **Legal Obligation:** To meet tax, accounting, regulatory, law enforcement, or other legal requirements.

## 4. How We Share Information

We may share personal information to the extent necessary with the following parties:

- Other users on the platform, such as when displaying profiles, briefs, applications, communications, and collaboration management;
- Service providers that help us operate, host, maintain, secure, analyze, and optimize our services;
- Payment, settlement, invoicing, tax, and billing service providers;
- Login, email, SMS, customer service, cloud, AI, analytics, and advertising service providers;
- Legal, accounting, audit, insurance, and other professional advisors;
- Regulators, courts, law enforcement agencies, or other parties legally entitled to request information;
- Parties involved in financing, mergers, restructuring, asset transfers, or similar transactions.

We do not charge third parties for the purpose of selling personal information itself. If certain advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies are considered "selling," "sharing," or "targeted advertising" under applicable law, we will provide opt-out mechanisms as required by law.

## 5. Cross-Border Processing

Overseed is a cross-border platform. Your personal information may be processed outside your country or region, including in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, China, Singapore, or other regions where our service providers are located.

Where required by applicable law, we will implement appropriate safeguards, such as data processing agreements, contractual protections, standard contractual clauses, transfer assessments, separate consent, or other lawful cross-border transfer mechanisms.

## 6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, maintain login sessions, remember preferences, analyze traffic, optimize services, and support marketing.

In regions where consent is required by law, we will obtain your consent before using non-essential cookies. You can manage your preferences through the Cookie banner, Cookie settings, or browser settings.

## 7. Marketing Communications

We may send you necessary notifications related to your account and services, such as registration, login, security, collaboration, payment, feature changes, or system notifications.

We may also send you marketing emails, event information, or product updates where permitted by law. You can unsubscribe at any time through the unsubscribe link in emails or by contacting us. Even if you unsubscribe from marketing, we may still send you necessary service notifications.

## 8. Information Retention

We will retain personal information for the reasonable period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including the periods needed to provide services, operate the business, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and enforce agreements.

When information is no longer needed, we will delete it, anonymize it, or securely store it as permitted by law.

## 9. Information Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including access controls, permission management, data transmission protection, logging, security monitoring, and necessary internal management measures.

However, no website, system, or network transmission can guarantee absolute security. You should also safeguard your own account and password.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on the applicable laws in your region, you may have the following rights:

- Access or inquire about personal information we hold about you;
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
- Delete certain personal information;
- Restrict or object to certain processing activities;
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
- Request export of certain personal information;
- Unsubscribe from marketing communications;
- Opt out of certain advertising, sale, sharing, or targeted advertising processing where applicable.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@overseed.net. To protect account security, we may need to verify your identity.
