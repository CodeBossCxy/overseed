# Overseed Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026

At Overseed (overseed.net), we believe in being clear and open about how we collect and use data related to you. In the spirit of transparency, this policy provides detailed information about how and when we use cookies and similar technologies on our Platform.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies & Similar Technologies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) by websites that you visit. They allow us to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

In addition to cookies, we also use the following similar technologies:
*   **Local Storage:** To store session data directly on your browser for better performance.
*   **Web Beacons / Pixels:** Tiny graphics with a unique identifier that allow us to understand whether you have visited a certain page or opened an email.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

Overseed uses several categories of cookies to provide, protect, and improve our services:

*   **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Essential for the Platform to function (e.g., session management, security, authentication).
*   **Functional Cookies:** To remember choices you make (such as your language preference or region).
*   **Analytics & Performance Cookies:** To help us understand how visitors interact with Overseed.
*   **Third-Party Cookies:** Integrated with services such as Google OAuth and WeChat OAuth.

## 4. Google Analytics Consent Mode (UK Users)

For users in the United Kingdom, we comply with **Google Analytics Consent Mode v2**. Google Analytics is only activated after you provide explicit consent via our cookie banner. If consent is refused, we will only collect anonymized, non-identifying data in accordance with Google's consent mode framework.

## 5. Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. 

*   **Browser Controls:** Most web browsers allow you to block all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being set through their settings.
*   **Consent Banner:** For users in the UK, EEA, and other jurisdictions with mandatory consent requirements, we provide a cookie consent banner upon your first visit, allowing you to opt-in or opt-out of non-essential cookies.
*   **Impact of Disabling:** Disabling strictly necessary cookies may cause parts of the Platform to function incorrectly.

## 6. Legal Bases for Processing

*   **UK GDPR & PECR:** We rely on **Legitimate Interest** for strictly necessary cookies. For all functional and analytics cookies, we rely on your **Consent**.
*   **China (PIPL):** We obtain explicit consent where required for the collection of non-essential identifiers.
