## 3. Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information from and about users of our Platform:

### A. Account Data
*   **Registration Info:** Name, email address, and hashed password.
*   **Profile Photo:** Images uploaded to your user profile.
*   **Third-Party Login:** If you choose to sign in via Google OAuth or WeChat OAuth, we receive profile information (name, profile picture) and your email address from these providers.

### B. Creator Profile Data (for Influencers/Creators)
*   **Social Media Identifiers:** Social media handles and profile links.
*   **Metrics:** Follower counts, engagement rates, and audience demographics.
*   **Content Samples:** Examples of previous work and content style.

### C. Brand/Merchant Data (for Brands/Merchants)
*   **Business Info:** Company name, business category, and contact details.
*   **Campaign Specifics:** Campaign briefs, goals, and requirements.
*   **Product Info:** Details regarding products intended for promotion.

### D. Payment & Transaction Data
*   **Financial Details:** Bank account numbers or other payment details for settlement.
*   *Note:* Payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
*   **Sensitive Personal Information (PIPL):** Financial data is classified as sensitive personal information under China's PIPL. We will obtain your **separate and explicit consent** before collecting and processing such data, and will only process it for the specific purpose of facilitating payment settlement.

### E. Communication Data
*   **Messages:** Direct messages exchanged between brands and creators on the Platform, including AI-generated translations of these messages.

### F. Usage & Technical Data
*   **Identifiers:** IP address, device type, browser type, and operating system.
*   **Activity:** Pages visited, timestamps, clickstream data, and time spent on the Platform.
*   **Cookies & Similar Technologies:** Data collected through cookies, local storage, pixels, and web beacons. See our [Cookie Policy](https://overseed.net/cookie-policy) for details.

### G. AI Interaction Data
*   **Queries:** Questions or prompts sent to our AI assistant.
*   **Preferences:** Matching preferences used by our AI algorithms to suggest collaborations.
*   **AI Content:** User inputs used for AI-generated content or e-commerce detail page generation.

### H. Campaign Data
*   **Performance:** Collaboration records, content submission status, and campaign performance metrics.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

### A. UK GDPR & EEA Users
If you are located in the UK or EEA, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

*   **Performance of a Contract:** To fulfill our obligations under the Terms of Service.
*   **Consent:** Where you have given clear consent for a specific purpose (e.g., marketing communications, non-essential cookies).
*   **Legitimate Interests:** For our business interests in improving the Platform, ensuring security, and facilitating efficient matching, provided these do not override your rights.
*   **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable laws (e.g., tax or anti-money laundering regulations).

### B. China PIPL Legal Bases (Article 13)
If you are located in China, we process your personal information under the following legal bases:

*   **Consent:** Processing based on your voluntary, explicit, and informed consent.
*   **Contract Necessity:** Processing necessary for the conclusion or performance of a contract to which you are a party.
*   **Legal Obligation:** Processing necessary for the performance of statutory duties or obligations.
*   **Public Interest:** Processing for the performance of duties in the public interest, such as news reporting or public health.
*   **Reasonable Scope:** Processing of personal information already publicly disclosed by you, within a reasonable scope.

## 6. AI and Automated Decision-Making

Overseed uses AI-driven algorithms to create profiles and suggest matches between creators and brands.

*   **Profiling:** We process creator data (metrics, demographics) to categorize and rank suitability for specific campaigns.
*   **Automated Suggestions:** While our AI suggests matches, the final decision to enter into a collaboration remains with the users.
*   **Human Review:** Under the UK GDPR (Art. 22) and China PIPL, you have the right to request an explanation of automated decisions that significantly affect you, or to request human intervention. You may also refuse to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing. Contact us at privacy@overseed.net to exercise these rights.

## 7. Sharing & Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We disclose your information only as follows:

*   **Collaboration Partners:** When you enter a collaboration, we share necessary profile and contact data with the other party (brand or creator).
*   **Service Providers:** We share data with cloud hosting providers, AI infrastructure providers, payment processors, and analytics services who work on our behalf under contractual obligations to protect your data.
*   **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Overseed or our users.
*   **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as a business asset. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 8. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Overseed is operated from China. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that your personal information will be transferred to and processed in China.

*   **From the UK/EEA:** We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other recognized safeguards approved by the UK Secretary of State or European Commission to ensure that transfers to China provide an adequate level of protection.
*   **From China:** We comply with the China Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) regarding outbound data transfers, including performing necessary security assessments or entering into standard contracts with overseas recipients where required by the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC).
*   **From Canada:** We comply with PIPEDA requirements to ensure that any personal information transferred to a third party for processing is afforded a comparable level of protection by way of contractual or other means.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services.

*   **Account Closure:** Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except for data we are required to retain by law (typically up to 7 years for tax and accounting records).
*   **Anonymization:** We may retain anonymized or aggregated data indefinitely for research and platform improvement. Anonymized data is not personal information.

*   **Notify Authorities:** Report the breach to the relevant supervisory authorities without undue delay (within 72 hours for UK GDPR; as required under PIPL and applicable US state laws).

## 11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your personal information.

### A. Rights for All Users
*   **Access:** The right to request copies of your personal data.
*   **Correction:** The right to request that we correct inaccurate information.
*   **Deletion:** The right to request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.

### B. China PIPL Rights
*   **Withdrawal of Consent:** You may withdraw consent for processing at any time; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.
*   **Right to Copy:** You may request a copy of your personal information (Art. 45).
*   **Right to Portability:** You may request the transfer of your personal information to another personal information handler you designate, provided it meets CAC conditions (Art. 45).
*   **Automated Decision Explanation:** The right to request an explanation of how our AI makes decisions about you, and the right to refuse decisions made solely through automated decision-making.
*   **Deregistration:** The right to cancel your account and have your data deleted.
*   **Posthumous Rights:** Your close relatives may exercise your rights after your passing (Art. 49).

### C. UK GDPR Rights
*   **Object/Restrict:** The right to object to processing or request restriction of processing.
*   **Data Portability:** The right to request that we transfer your data to another organization in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
*   **Right Not to be Subject to Automated Decisions:** The right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.
*   **Right to Complain:** You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
*   **Response Time:** We will respond to your request within **one calendar month**. This period may be extended by up to two additional months for complex or numerous requests, in which case we will inform you of the extension.

### D. US State Privacy Laws (CCPA/CPRA, etc.)
*   **Right to Know:** You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
*   **Right to Delete:** You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
*   **Right to Correct:** You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
*   **Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing:** We do **not** sell your personal data. You have the right to opt out of the "sharing" of personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may exercise this right through the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on our website (where applicable).
*   **Limit Sensitive PI:** You may request that we limit the use of sensitive personal information to only what is necessary for the service.
*   **Authorized Agent:** You may designate an authorized agent to make privacy requests on your behalf. We may require proof of authorization and identity verification.
*   **Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
*   **Response Time:** We will respond to verified consumer requests within **45 calendar days**. This period may be extended by an additional 45 days where reasonably necessary, in which case we will notify you.

### E. Canada PIPEDA Rights
*   **Access/Correction:** You may access your personal information held by us and challenge the accuracy and completeness of it.
*   **Withdrawal of Consent:** You may withdraw consent for the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual restrictions and reasonable notice.
*   **Privacy Commissioner:** You have the right to complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC).
*   **Response Time:** We will respond to your request within **30 days**.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **privacy@overseed.net**. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
